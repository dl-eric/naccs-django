COLLEGIATE HUB

WHAT IS THE HUB?

Our collegiate hub is built around the collegiate community. Since its creation, students and graduates have come together to have fun and play CS:GO. No matter what skill level, any current or graduated student is welcome to join in!

Don't think you're that good? No worries! Join the hub to learn from your peers.

Think you're one of the best? Represent your school amongst the hundreds of other students playing!

Check the hub out here: FACEIT Hub

HOW TO JOIN

1. Create an account

2. Verify your college credentials

3. Link your Accounts

4. Generate your invite link!

DISCORD

As a final step, every player is required to join our Discord! Once you're in, be sure to head over to #verify-me to verify your account. We use Discord for all our hub matches.

PRIZES

Prizes for this hub have been historically cash, coupons, and FACEIT points for the top of the leaderboard. We also do random giveaways for whoever's played, too.

Keep a lookout in Discord for updates on our hub prizing!
